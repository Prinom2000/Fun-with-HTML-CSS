Why East West University?

East West University offers world-class degree programs taught by experts, and is committed to providing each and every student with the guidance and support they need to succeed.

Here are just a few of the reasons why you should choose East West University:

  • Affordable tuition
  • Convenient location in Chicago's South Loop neighborhood
  • 17-story Student Life Center featuring multiple gyms, basketball courts, computer labs, and an expansive library
  • A wide range of academic programs to choose from
  • Experienced faculty who are passionate about teaching
  • Supportive staff who are dedicated to helping students succeed
